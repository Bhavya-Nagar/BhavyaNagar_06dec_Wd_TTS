Q.14) What is the use of a span tag? Explain with example?

A) Actually the use of a span tag is very simple and easy because if we want to apply a style on a specific part of group of elements at that we can use a span tag. 


Example of span tag are as follows:-

<p>This is a <span style="color:red;">bhavya</span>nagar.</p>


Thus is the use of a span tag and example
